Platform Features

Everything You Need for Effective Volunteer Management

From smart AI matching to real-time emergency response, Sahaayak has it all.

Smart AI Matching

Our scoring algorithm matches volunteers based on skill overlap, proximity, and availability — ensuring the right person gets every task.

Score = (Skill×5) + (Distance×3) + (Availability×2)

Live Map View

Visualize volunteer locations and task sites on an interactive map. Find nearest volunteers instantly.

Emergency Mode

One toggle activates emergency mode — instantly notifying all nearby volunteers and prioritizing critical tasks.

Real-time Alerts

WebSocket-powered notifications keep volunteers and admins in sync — task assignments, status updates, and emergencies.

Analytics Dashboard

Track volunteer activity, task completion rates, average response times, and skill demand with beautiful charts.

Manual Override

Admins can always override AI suggestions and manually assign specific volunteers when needed.

How It Works

From Signup to Impact in 4 Simple Steps

01

Volunteer Signs Up

Set your profile with skills, availability, and location. Go online when ready to help.

02

Admin Creates Task

NGO admins post tasks with required skills, urgency level, and location details.

03

Smart Auto-Assign

The AI engine scores all available volunteers and assigns the best match instantly.

04

Task Completed

Volunteer accepts, performs the task, marks it complete. Analytics updated in real time.
